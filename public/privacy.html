Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

AdsTable is a read-only advertising analytics dashboard for small and mid-sized e-commerce advertisers.

1. What AdsTable Does

AdsTable allows users to connect their own advertising accounts in order to view campaign performance data in one dashboard.

AdsTable may display metrics such as spend, impressions, clicks, CTR, CPC, conversions, ROAS, campaign names, ad account names and related advertising performance data.

AdsTable does not create, edit, pause, delete or manage advertising campaigns. AdsTable only reads advertising performance data for reporting and analytics purposes.

2. Data We Access

When a user connects an advertising platform, AdsTable may request read-only access to advertising data, including:

  • Ad account information
  • Campaign performance data
  • Campaign names
  • Spend
  • Impressions
  • Clicks
  • Conversions
  • Revenue or conversion value, when provided by the platform

AdsTable only accesses data that the user explicitly authorizes through the connected platform’s OAuth authorization flow.

3. How We Use Data

We use connected advertising data only to display advertising performance, provide reporting and analytics, compare performance across advertising platforms, and generate summarized insights about campaign performance.

We do not sell user advertising data.

4. Data Sharing

AdsTable does not sell, rent or share user advertising data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Data may be processed by infrastructure providers used to operate the application, such as hosting and database providers.

5. User Control

Users can disconnect their advertising accounts at any time from the connected platform or from AdsTable when account management features are available.

6. Security

AdsTable is designed to use read-only access wherever possible. Access tokens and related credentials should be stored securely and used only to retrieve authorized reporting data.

7. Contact

For privacy questions or data access requests, contact: hello@adstable.app

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as AdsTable evolves. Updates will be posted on this page.
